# BitRent Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: March 15, 2026  
**Effective Date**: [To be set at launch]

## 1. Introduction

BitRent ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the BitRent platform. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information**: Nostr public key, profile information
- **Transaction Data**: Rental agreements, payment history, mining activity
- **Communication**: Messages, support requests, feedback
- **Device Information**: Browser type, IP address, operating system

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, search queries
- **Technical Data**: Timestamps, error logs, API usage
- **Location Data**: Approximate location from IP address (with your consent)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Payment Providers**: Transaction details from Stripe/payment processors
- **Analytics**: Data from Google Analytics, Sentry
- **Social Media**: Data if you link accounts

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

### Primary Purposes
1. **Service Delivery**
   - Providing the BitRent rental platform
   - Processing transactions
   - Managing user accounts
   - Customer support

2. **Improvement & Analytics**
   - Analyzing platform usage
   - Fixing bugs and improving features
   - Performance monitoring
   - Understanding user needs

3. **Security & Compliance**
   - Fraud detection and prevention
   - Account security
   - Legal compliance
   - Abuse prevention

4. **Communication**
   - Service updates
   - Important notices
   - Customer support responses
   - Promotional content (with consent)

### Legal Bases (GDPR)
- **Contract**: To perform our service obligations
- **Legitimate Interest**: To improve services, prevent fraud, ensure security
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with laws
- **Consent**: For marketing and optional data collection

## 4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do NOT Share Data With:
- ❌ Advertisers
- ❌ Data brokers
- ❌ Third-party marketers
- ❌ Unaffiliated companies (without consent)

### 4.2 We May Share Data With:
- **Service Providers**: Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics
- **Legal Requirements**: Law enforcement, court orders (when required)
- **Business Partners**: Only with explicit user consent
- **Aggregate Data**: Non-identifying, anonymized statistics

### 4.3 Data Processing Agreements
All service providers must sign Data Processing Agreements (DPA) ensuring:
- Proper data security
- Limited data usage
- Compliance with regulations

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
- ✅ AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- ✅ TLS 1.2+ for data in transit
- ✅ Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Penetration testing
- ✅ Intrusion detection

### 5.2 What We Don't Store
- ❌ Passwords in plain text
- ❌ Credit card numbers (Stripe handles this)
- ❌ Private keys
- ❌ Unnecessary personal data

### 5.3 Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach:
1. We'll investigate immediately
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours
3. Notify authorities as required
4. Provide guidance on protection measures

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)
- ✅ **Right to Access**: Request copy of your data
- ✅ **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- ✅ **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- ✅ **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use data
- ✅ **Right to Data Portability**: Export your data
- ✅ **Right to Object**: Opt-out of processing
- ✅ **Right to Lodge a Complaint**: With your local DPA

### 6.2 California CCPA Rights
- ✅ Right to know what personal information is collected
- ✅ Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- ✅ Right to delete personal information
- ✅ Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- ✅ Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### 6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights
Email: privacy@bitrent.io with:
- "Right to Access", "Right to Deletion", etc.
- Your user ID or email
- Verification of identity

We'll respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA).
